Special Features Inclusive with Mould

  • We design preform according to clients' specifications.
  • Channels for cooling water are designed to flow turbulently.
  • Assembled stack parts for the hot runner system sourced internationally.
  • The mould is designed for fully automatic operation with tail-free components, pneumatic shut-off valves, and a balanced hot runner system.
  • We use Unbrako or TVS fasteners.
  • Mould material specification
    Cavity: DIN 1.2316 ESR - Bohler (German Make), corrosion resistance steel with vacuum hardening up to 46-48 HRC and grinding for long life.
    Core: DIN 1.2316 ESR - Bohler (German Make), corrosion resistance steel.
    Neck Ring: DIN 1.2316 ESR with vacuum hardening up to 45-48 HRC and grinding for long life.
    Hot Runner Plates: 1.2085 corrosion resistance steel.
    Mould base plates: P20 (1.2311), pre-hardened with 30 HRC hardness.
    All mould base plates are electroless nickel plated (ENP).
    Advanced profile heaters are used for smooth mould operation start-up.
